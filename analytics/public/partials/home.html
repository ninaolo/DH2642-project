Welcome to Meeter

Meeter is an office platform for building meeting agendas.
Share your agendas with colleagues and connect to Google Calendar.

Login Register


What can you do on meeter?

Plan meetings with your colleagues

Meeter is specifically made to be used in an office environment since it enables easy sharing and inviting of colleagues to events. Using prepared agendas

Connect to Google Calendar

Meeter automaticall connects to Google Calender and adds an agenda to the users schedule; making it easy to view and find upcoming events using the web application or mobile app.

Become more productive

Meeting attendees can familiarize themselves with the meeting agenda before the meeting and prepare material or information needed. Meeter also makes it possible for colleagues to edit and collaborate on an agenda's activities and specifications together.
