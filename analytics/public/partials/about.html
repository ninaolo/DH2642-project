#1 What is meeter?


Meeter is a platform made for planning and creating meeting agendas. Meeter is specifically made to be used in an office environment since it enables easy sharing and inviting of colleagues. Meeter also supports collaboration among the invited colleagues of an agenda.

#2 How do I add an agenda?


Adding a new agenda is found in the personal menu in the header. An agenda consists of customized activities that are easily added to an agenda by drag and drop. From this view the user continues to and is able to invite other colleagues.

#3 What is Google Calendar?


Google Calendar is a time-management web application and mobile app created by Google. Meeter automatically connects to Google Calender and adds an agenda to the users schedule; making it easy for the users to view and find upcoming events through the web and their mobile device.
